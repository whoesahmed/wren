Built for the curious.

Six reasons WREN gets you from question to understanding faster than anything else.

Simplify 'Complex' topics

Type any topic and WREN fetches a clean, readable summary directly from Wikipedia's API, no ads, no clutter, no waiting.

Research 'Oriented'

WREN is a research navigator built to bridge the gap between raw information and true understanding. By organizing Wikipedia content into structured insights, WREN empowers students, analysts, and professionals to conduct research with confidence and efficiency.

Text-to-'Speech'

Every answer is read aloud automatically. Perfect for multitasking, learning on the go, or accessibility.

Startup & 'Innovative'

WREN is more than a navigator. It’s your smart research companion. By blending intuitive design with intelligent search, WREN helps you move beyond endless scrolling to meaningful understanding. Knowledge shouldn’t be overwhelming; WREN makes it simple..

Professional & 'Corporate'

WREN is designed for professionals who need to conduct thorough research quickly and efficiently. Its clean interface and powerful search capabilities make it an ideal tool for corporate environments.

Mobile Ready

Fully responsive across all screen sizes. Works as a progressive web app you can install on your home screen.
